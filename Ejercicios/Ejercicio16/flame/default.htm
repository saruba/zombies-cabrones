 Update v3.0: November 11, 2000
Ok, one last update for this applet to make this the best and most useful flame applet out there.
  1. Now you can change the size of the font, probably one of the more useful options.
  2. Now you can turn off the "bottom flame" (the stove) and just have the burning words.
  3. Now you can change the vertical position of the text.
  4. Add a new Color # 6 Rose Pink.
 Update v2.0: October 6, 2000
This will probably be the final update to the popular flame applet. Here are some new feature in this version
  1. Copyright note on the bottom removed and replaced by a small link box that only appears when mouse is over the applet.
  2. 5 new colors to select from:
    1. fire (default)
    2. green
    3. ash blue
    4. midnight purple
    5. black & white
  3. New brighter fire color
  4. Change the flame color in realtime. (go over the lower right corner of the applet to find the button)
 Update v1.1: August 5,1997
